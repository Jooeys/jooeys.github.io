Who we are?

As part of a partnership with Issy-les-Moulineaux, We are students of ISEP (Institut supérieur d’électronique de Paris based in particular in Issy) worked on open data for the city and INSEE.

From our work on the data,Junyi Zhong, Charlotte Bruzeau, Anis Hbaieb, Chaojun Zhang and Yuan Zhou have created data visualizations gathered in a dashboard that reveal the demographic evolution of the city's population over the years. time and age groups, but also education level, travel flows, gender equality at work, etc.
